    Who are we?

    The Bridge Comedy Club plays host to the best comedy Tuesdays-Saturdays in Newcastle and Middlesbrough, earning its place as Best Newcastle and Middlesbrough Comedy Club year-on-year

    Some of the comedians that have played at our shows include

    • Jack Whitehall
    • Ricky Gervais
    • Eddie Murphy
    • Chris Rock

    Not only do we host these world class comedians but also upcoming and new comedians such as Cher Griffiths and Duncan Skilton.

    How do you book an event?

    Browse upcoming events

    Go to out event page and browse through our upcoming events

    Pick an event

    Once you've browsed through our events yo can then pick one

    Book your event

    Once you've picked your event, you can then go ahead and book it

    Receive Confirmation

    Once you've booked your event, you will then receive a confirmation e-mail from us with all your details and your ticket

    Relax and look forward to the show

    You can now sit back and relax while you wait for your event

    Enjoy your day

    Come to your event, and enjoy your time. Take photos and have fun!
